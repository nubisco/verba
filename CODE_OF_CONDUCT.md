# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the Verba project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments in any form
- Publishing private information without consent
- Trolling or sustained disruption of discussions
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behaviour may be reported to **[conduct@nubisco.io](mailto:conduct@nubisco.io)**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality for reporters.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct may be temporarily or permanently removed from the project.
